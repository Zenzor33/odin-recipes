Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add salt
  3. Carefully add eggs to the boiling water
  4. Reduce to a simmer
  5. Cook for 8 to 12 minutes, depending on desired taste
  6. Remove eggs to a bowl of ice water. Let sit for 15 minutes
  7. Peel & Eat!
